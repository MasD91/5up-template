Work

With an accumulated experience of over 25 years in the construction industry, our company, led by two dedicated brothers, excels in a variety of loft conversions. Our expertise encompasses dormer loft conversions, providing additional headroom and versatility by extending the roof vertically. We specialize in hip to gable loft conversions, seamlessly transforming hipped roofs into gables to maximize space. Mansard loft conversions, characterized by a flat roof with a steep slope, are also within our repertoire, offering an elegant and spacious solution. Additionally, our team is proficient in roof lift loft conversions, elevating your roof to create more room without altering the external structure. These diverse approaches showcase our commitment to tailoring loft conversions to the specific needs and preferences of our clients.

With a proven track record in executing a range of loft conversion types, our collective experience enables us to navigate the intricacies of each project. From the design intricacies of dormer conversions to the structural adjustments required for mansard transformations, our team ensures precision and innovation at every step. Whether you seek an expanded living space, a stylish retreat, or a functional home office, our expertise in dormer, hip to gable, mansard, and roof lift loft conversions positions us as a reliable partner to bring your vision to life.

Request a Quote:

Begin the process by visiting the contact page. You will find a form designed to capture essential details about the work you wish to be done. Complete all required fields accurately to ensure a comprehensive understanding of your project requirements.

Site Survey:

Following your quote request, our team will conduct a site survey to assess specific project needs and gather additional insights. This step allows us to better understand the unique aspects of your project, ensuring that our services are tailored to meet your exact specifications.

Quote/Proforma:

Once the site survey is complete, our team will generate a detailed quote or proforma invoice based on the information provided. This document will outline the scope of work, associated costs, and any other relevant details. Review the quote/proforma carefully and feel free to reach out if you have any questions or require further clarification.

Starting Work:

Upon acceptance of the quote/proforma, the final step is to confirm your decision to proceed. Once confirmed, our team will commence the agreed-upon work according to the outlined schedule and project specifications. Regular communication will be maintained throughout the process to ensure your satisfaction and address any concerns that may arise during the project.
